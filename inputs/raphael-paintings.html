About

Description

Raffaello Sanzio da Urbino, now generally known in English as Raphael, was an Italian painter and architect of the High Renaissance. His work is admired for its clarity of form, ease of composition, and visual achievement of the Neoplatonic ideal of human grandeur. Wikipedia
Born: 1483, Urbino, Italy
Died: April 6, 1520 (age 37 years), Rome, Italy
Periods: Renaissance, Italian Renaissance, High Renaissance
Influenced by: Michelangelo, Leonardo da Vinci, Albrecht Dürer, Melozzo da Forlì
Influenced: Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres · See more
Full name: Raffaello Sanzio
